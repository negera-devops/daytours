Overview

Explore Sri Lanka's most famous tropical rainforest with our Sinharaja Rainforest Tour from Galle - a private nature day trip for travelers who want to experience rich biodiversity, guided jungle trekking, endemic wildlife, and peaceful rainforest scenery in one unforgettable journey. Sinharaja is one of Sri Lanka's most important natural treasures and a UNESCO World Heritage Site - a pristine wet-zone rainforest home to endemic birds, rare butterflies, giant trees, freshwater streams, and a remarkable natural ecosystem found nowhere else on earth. With an optional village lunch and a scenic drive through tea country and lush countryside, this is the finest nature escape from the southern coast.

  • Guided trekking experience through Sinharaja Rainforest - a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Discover endemic birds, butterflies, reptiles, rare plants, and giant tropical trees
  • One of the most biodiverse rainforests in Asia - ideal for birdwatchers and nature lovers
  • Scenic drive from Galle through countryside, villages, and tea-growing landscapes
  • Optional authentic Sri Lankan lunch served at a local village home
