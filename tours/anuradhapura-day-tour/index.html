Overview

Visiting Anuradhapura means stepping into the first great kingdom of Sri Lanka — a sprawling ancient city that flourished for over a thousand years and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The scale of the ruins is immense, and navigating them without a guide means missing the stories that bring the stones to life. Prebook your full-day tour to include hotel pickup and a specialist history guide.

  • Visit the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi — a 2,300-year-old fig tree revered worldwide
  • Explore colossal dagobas including Ruwanwelisaya and Jetavanaramaya
  • Walk through the royal palace ruins and ancient bathing pools
  • Specialist history guide included to bring the ancient city to life
  • Bicycle hire available to cover the vast UNESCO World Heritage Site
