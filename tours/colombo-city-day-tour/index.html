Overview

Colombo is a city of contrasts — colonial-era architecture stands alongside gleaming skyscrapers, while fragrant spice markets sit just minutes from serene Buddhist temples. A guided city day tour is the best way to navigate this vibrant capital without the hassle of figuring out routes and timings on your own.

  • Explore colonial landmarks including the Old Parliament and Galle Face Hotel
  • Visit the Gangaramaya Buddhist Temple and Kelaniya Raja Maha Vihara
  • Browse the bustling Pettah Market for spices, textiles, and street food
  • Stroll along Galle Face Green with views of the Indian Ocean
  • Hotel pickup, drop-off, and a private guide included
